Extrinsic v Intrinsic Rewards

Broadly, there are two types of "rewards" or reasons teachers are teachers: Extrinsic, aka external rewards, and instrinic, or internal rewards

    Extrinsic
  • Salary
  • Higher status (everyone needs to be taught, and teaching is very noble)
  • Power
  • Work Schedule (summer's off!)
    Intrinsic
  • Enjoyable profession / teaching in of itself
  • Teaching is for the better good
  • Knowing other teachers
  • Joy of the process
